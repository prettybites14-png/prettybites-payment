Delivery Policy

Pretty Bites offers pickup and delivery options based on product type, location, and weekly availability.

Delivery Area

Delivery is available in Windsor, Ontario and selected surrounding areas. Some areas may require additional notice or fees.

Delivery Fees

Delivery fees are calculated at checkout. Weekly meal plans may include a delivery fee per day, depending on the options you select.

Timing

Delivery times are estimated and can vary. Please ensure someone is available to receive the order at the provided address and preferred time window.

Pickup Orders

Pickup orders must be collected at the agreed time. If you need to adjust your pickup, contact us as soon as possible.
